Who we work with

01

Architecture
& Interiors

Architects and interior designers use our CNC routing service to produce decorative screens, feature panels, bespoke joinery components, ceiling elements and anything else that needs to be cut with more precision than a handsaw can deliver.

We regularly produce large-volume runs of complex repeat patterns — the ornate lattice panels shown in our portfolio are a prime example. The same file, cut dozens of times, each one identical. That's what CNC does that manual fabrication can't.

If you're specifying CNC-cut components for a fit-out or interior project, send us the drawings. We'll quote by return.

Set & Stage
Design

Theatre, film, TV and events. We've produced large-scale gear assemblies, arch structures, decorative forms and structural set elements for professional productions.

Set designers and production managers work to tight deadlines with zero tolerance for error. We understand production schedules and we quote fast — typically within 24 hours of receiving your file.

Foam, MDF, plywood — whatever the set requires. If it's CNC-cuttable, we can produce it.
