About

Rule One CNC

Professional CNC machining, routing and cutting from our workshop in Walthamstow, East London. Operated by Rule One Productions.

Who we are

Built for
professional work

Rule One CNC is the CNC machining and fabrication arm of Rule One Productions — a creative production company based in Walthamstow, East London. We've been cutting, routing and milling for architects, set designers, fabricators, product designers and production companies for years.

We run a Pegasus 1530 ATC — a professional-grade CNC router with a 1500×3000mm bed, 9kW Italian HSD spindle, Yaskawa servo motors and a 12-tool automatic tool changer. It's not a hobbyist machine. It's built for the kind of work our clients bring us.

Our workshop is at 22 Rigg Approach, Walthamstow, E10 7QN. Jobs can be collected from the workshop or sent by courier. We serve clients across London and the South East.

What sets us apart

Serious kit.

Professional machine

The Pegasus 1530 ATC is a commercial-grade router. 9kW HSD spindle, Yaskawa servos, 12-tool ATC. Not a hobby machine — built for the work architects, production companies and fabricators actually need.

No minimum order

We cut one part as readily as a thousand. Single prototypes, short runs, full production batches — same machine, same precision, same service.

Fast, honest quoting

Send your file and job details. We confirm cost within 24 hours on standard jobs. No vague estimates, no hidden setup fees. A price you can plan around.

East London based

Walthamstow, E10. A short hop from central London. Workshop collection available, or we arrange courier delivery. Real people, real workshop, real work.

Creative production roots

Rule One Productions brings a creative production background to every job. We understand design intent, tight deadlines and the difference between a good cut and a great one.

Deep material range

MDF to aluminium, foam to hardwood, acrylic to engineering plastics. Up to 70mm in a single pass. For thicker builds, we develop a layered cutting plan.
