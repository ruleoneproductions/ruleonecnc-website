Service 01

CNC Routing
in London

High-speed 3-axis routing across our full 1500×3000mm bed. We route MDF, plywood, hardwood, foam, composites and a wide range of sheet materials to your exact file spec.

Used extensively for architectural panels, decorative screens, set design elements, interior joinery components and retail fixtures. Our 9kW HSD spindle handles everything from fine detail work to deep aggressive cuts in dense material.

Maximum cutting depth: 70mm in a single pass. For thicker builds, we develop a layered cutting and assembly plan.

CNC Milling
in London

Precision milling for aluminium, acrylic and engineering plastics. Where routing removes material quickly, milling works with tighter tolerances and harder materials.

Used for product prototypes, jigs, tooling plates, bespoke mechanical components and anything where dimensional accuracy is critical. Our Yaskawa servo motors deliver repeatable precision across every pass.

Service 04

3-Axis Machining
in London

Full 3-axis capability for relief carving, contoured surfaces and complex 3D forms. Where standard routing works in two dimensions, 3-axis machining lets the tool move through depth — producing curved, sculpted or tapered results from a single block or sheet.

Used for mould-making, architectural relief panels, sculptural set elements and any component that needs more than a flat cut. The 12-tool automatic changer means finishing passes with different tools run without manual changeover.
